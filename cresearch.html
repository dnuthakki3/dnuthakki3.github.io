Information Security Analyst

I am interested in the cybersecurity industry as I have been in this pathway throughout all of highschool so I decided to look into Information Security Analyst. This is an entry level job in the field of cybersecurity that provides a well-rounded experience for a first job in this sector of IT. The responsibilities of this job include:

  • Monitoring network breaches
  • Investigating, documenting, and reporting security breaches
  • Performing compliance control testing
  • Researching IT security trends
  • Helping computer users with security products and procedures
  • Developing strategies to help their organization remain secure

To be in a position of this kind, it requires a multitude of skills the top five of which includes:

  1. Security Management Framework
  2. Operating Systems Vulnerabilities
  3. Network Infastructure
  4. Client-facing Skills
  5. Business Acumen

As for the educational background to become an Informational Security Analyst, there are different paths that one can take. The more common approach is to get a bachelor's degree in computer science or informational technology or even a related field like math or engineering. That being said, some workers can find their way into this field by having a high school diploma along with relevant industry certifications and training. As for the certifications, some of the ones employers look for are the Certified Information Systems Security Professional(CISSP) and the CompTia Security+ certificates.
