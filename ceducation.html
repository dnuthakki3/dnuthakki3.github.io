Career-Related Education

I have undertaken many different opportunities to enrich my knowledge about cybersecurity and the industry as a whole to ensure that I have relevant knowledge in the field. Currently, I am learning the C and C++ languages to make a ransomware project. It has shown me how the principles of Object Oriented Programming that I learned in Java are held constant in all Object Oriented Programming languages. Additionally, by making the ransomware I am able to see exactly how hackers target certain weaknesses in operating systems. It has been a very enriching experience that has helped me work on both new languages and understand the behind the scenes work of how ransomware works.
